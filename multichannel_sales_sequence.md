# Multichannel Sales Sequence Outline

## Step 1: Initial Contact
- **Objective**: Introduce the product/service and capture interest.
- **Channels**: Email, Social Media, LinkedIn
- **Actions**: 
  - Send personalized email introducing the product/service.
  - Share engaging content on social media platforms.
  - Reach out via LinkedIn with a tailored message.

## Step 2: Qualification
- **Objective**: Determine the potential customer’s needs and fit.
- **Channels**: Phone, Email, Chatbots
- **Actions**: 
  - Schedule a phone call to discuss needs and challenges.
  - Send follow-up emails with relevant information.
  - Utilize chatbots for initial qualification.

## Step 3: Proposal
- **Objective**: Present a solution that aligns with the customer’s needs.
- **Channels**: Video Call, Email, Webinar
- **Actions**: 
  - Host a video call to present the proposal.
  - Send detailed proposal via email.
  - Conduct a webinar to showcase the solution.

## Step 4: Negotiation and Close
- **Objective**: Address concerns, negotiate terms, and close the deal.
- **Channels**: Email, Phone, Live Chat
- **Actions**: 
  - Address any concerns via email or phone.
  - Engage in live chat for immediate feedback and negotiation.
  - Finalize the agreement and close the sale.
