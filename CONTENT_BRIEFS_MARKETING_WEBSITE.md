# Content Briefs: Marketing Website MVP

## Brief 1: Homepage Hero & Value Proposition

### Objective
Create compelling above-the-fold content that immediately communicates Foreman's value proposition to ICP segments and drives signups.

### Target Audience
- Solopreneurs (primary): Time-strapped, technically curious but not experts
- Small Business Owners (secondary): 2-10 employees seeking operational efficiency

### Core Message
"Your AI Team, Ready in Minutes" - No-code AI agent deployment for business owners who need operational help but lack technical staff.

### Content Requirements

#### Hero Section:
```
Headline: Your AI Team, Ready in Minutes
Subheadline: For solopreneurs and small businesses who need AI agent capabilities but lack technical staff, Foreman is the AI agent orchestration platform that provisions and manages your AI team.

Key Benefits (bullet points):
• No-code deployment - Get AI working in 15 minutes, no technical expertise needed
• Managed operations - We handle infrastructure, monitoring, and optimization  
• Scalable team structure - Start with one agent, add specialists as you grow

Primary CTA: Start 14-Day Free Trial
Secondary CTA: See How It Works → (links to /how-it-works)

Trust Indicator: "No credit card required • SOC 2 compliant • GDPR ready"
```

#### Value Differentiation Section:
Title: How Foreman Is Different

Grid comparing:
1. **Foreman**: No-code, managed service, business-focused, 15-minute setup
2. **LangChain/CrewAI**: Coding required, self-managed, developer-focused, weeks to deploy
3. **Single AI Tools**: One function only, no coordination, fragmented workflows

Key differentiators to highlight:
- "Built for business owners, not AI engineers"
- "Managed infrastructure means reliability you can count on"
- "Multi-agent coordination for complete business workflows"

#### ICP-Focused Sections:
Create 3 columns targeting specific ICP segments:

1. **For Solopreneurs:**
   - Icon: 👨‍💼
   - Title: Swamped with operational tasks?
   - Pain points: "Wearing too many hats", "Limited time for growth", "Can't afford full-time help"
   - Solution: "Delegate to AI agents and focus on what only you can do"

2. **For Small Teams:**
   - Icon: 👥  
   - Title: Need to scale without hiring?
   - Pain points: "Inconsistent quality", "Manual processes eat time", "Hard to find good help"
   - Solution: "Extend your team's capacity with reliable AI agents"

3. **For Service Businesses:**
   - Icon: 💼
   - Title: Project work overwhelming?
   - Pain points: "Client communication overhead", "Quality varies", "Hard to scale capacity"
   - Solution: "Standardize delivery with AI-assisted workflows"

#### Proof Points Section:
Title: Built for Business Reliability

Grid with:
1. **Enterprise-Grade Foundation**: "Built on Paperclip, proven at scale"
2. **Production-Ready Runtime**: "Powered by OpenClaw for reliable execution"
3. **Multi-Provider Flexibility**: "Works with all major AI models via OpenRouter"
4. **Cost Control Options**: "BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) to reduce expenses"

### Design Requirements
- Clean, professional business tool aesthetic
- Mobile-responsive layout
- Clear visual hierarchy with primary CTA prominence
- Consistent iconography and illustration style
- Screenshots of actual product interface (not mockups)

### Success Metrics
- Conversion rate > 5% visitor to signup
- Time on page > 2 minutes
- Bounce rate < 40%

## Brief 3: How It Works Page

### Objective
Demonstrate the simplicity and effectiveness of Foreman's workflow, building confidence in the user experience.

### Target Audience
- Skeptical evaluators needing proof of simplicity
- Visual learners who prefer seeing the process
- Time-pressed visitors wanting quick understanding

### Core Message
"Simple four-step process from idea to execution" - See how easy it is to deploy and manage AI agents.

### Content Requirements

#### Visual Workflow Section:
Create 4-step visual flow with screenshots:

**Step 1: Describe Your Need**
- Visual: Chat interface screenshot
- Headline: "Tell us what you need help with"
- Description: "Plain English description of the task or workflow"
- Example: "I need help with weekly customer outreach emails"
- Outcome: "Foreman analyzes and proposes the right agent"

**Step 2: Review Agent Plan**
- Visual: Approval interface screenshot
- Headline: "Review and approve the plan"
- Description: "Your Chief of Staff agent creates a detailed execution plan"
- Example: "Marketing outreach agent with email templates and scheduling"
- Outcome: "You stay in control with human oversight"

**Step 3: Watch It Execute**
- Visual: Task execution dashboard
- Headline: "Monitor progress in real-time"
- Description: "Agent executes tasks autonomously with full transparency"
- Example: "Emails sent, responses tracked, metrics reported"
- Outcome: "Work gets done while you focus on strategy"

**Step 4: Stay in Control**
- Visual: Inbox/oversight interface
- Headline: "Approve key decisions as needed"
- Description: "Important choices come to your inbox for review"
- Example: "Unusual customer response needs your input"
- Outcome: "AI handles routine, you handle exceptions"

#### Real Results Section:
Title: What Our Customers Achieve

Metric cards showing:
- "90%+ task completion rate"
- "10-20 hours saved per week"
- "15-minute average setup time"
- "24/7 operational coverage"

2. **Research Assistant Agent:**
   - Problem: "Market research is time-consuming and inconsistent"

3. **Operations Coordinator Agent:**
   - Problem: "Project management overhead eats into billable time"
   - Solution: "AI agent tracks tasks, follows up, updates clients"
   - Result: "Clients stay informed without manual updates"

### Design Requirements
- Clear visual flow with numbered steps
- Actual product screenshots (not mockups)
- Consistent spacing and alignment
- Mobile-responsive step-by-step layout
- Interactive elements (hover states, animations)

### Success Metrics
- Time on page > 4 minutes (indicating thorough review)
- Video demo views > 30% of page visitors
