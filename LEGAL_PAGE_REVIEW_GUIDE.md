## 🆘 **COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS**

### **Q: What if I'm not a legal expert?**
**A:** Focus on business perspective: Are claims accurate? Is risk appropriately managed? Would you feel comfortable as a customer?

### **Q: What if pages use template language?**
**A:** That's common. Ensure template is appropriate for our business model (SaaS, AI, data processing).

### **Q: What's the biggest risk to watch for?**
**A:** Overpromising (guarantees we can't keep) or underprotecting (insufficient liability limitations).

### **Q: What if I have questions about specific clauses?**
**A:** Note them in your review. We can discuss or seek legal counsel if needed.
