## Personalization Guidelines

### Research Before Pitching
1. **Read their recent work:** Reference specific articles they've written
2. **Understand their audience:** Tailor angle to what resonates
3. **Check submission guidelines:** Follow publication requirements
4. **Find right contact:** Avoid generic "tips@" emails when possible

### Customization Points
**For each pitch, customize:**
- Opening line (reference their work)
- Angle (match their publication focus)
- Ask (what you want them to do)
- Timing (align with their editorial calendar)

**Example customizations:**
- **TechCrunch:** "Given your coverage of AI startups like [similar company]..."
- **Business Insider:** "Your readers who are small business owners would be interested in..."
- **Indie Hackers:** "As a fellow builder community, we wanted to share..."

### Avoid
- Generic mass emails
- Exaggerated claims ("revolutionary," "first ever")
- Long emails (keep under 200 words when possible)
- Attachments in first email (link instead)
- Following up too aggressively

1. Follow up once more
2. Move to nurture sequence
3. Try different contact at same publication
4. Consider different angle for re-pitch later
