# Week 1 Post-Launch Content Plan (April 29 - May 5, 2026)

## Overview
Detailed content plan for the first week after Foreman v2 public launch (April 28, 2026).

**Objective:** Maintain launch momentum, drive adoption, establish thought leadership, gather feedback  
**Theme:** "No-Code AI Agents in Action"  
**Audience:** New users, waitlist subscribers, broader AI/SMB community  
**Tone:** Educational, practical, results-focused, slightly enthusiastic

## Content Strategy

### Core Content Pillars
1. **Education:** How to use AI agents effectively
2. **Results:** Real outcomes and ROI examples
3. **Transparency:** Lessons learned, mistakes, improvements
4. **Community:** User stories, feedback, collaboration

### Distribution Strategy
- **Primary:** LinkedIn (daily posts + engagement)
- **Secondary:** Twitter/X (threads + real-time)
- **Tertiary:** Blog (detailed articles), Email (sequence), Community (engagement)
- **Amplification:** Paid promotion for top-performing content

## Content Formats & Specifications

### LinkedIn Posts
- **Format:** Native posts (not links)
- **Length:** 150-300 words ideal
- **Media:** Image (1080x1080), video (<90s), carousel (3-5 slides)
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant, mix of broad and niche
- **Timing:** 9:00 AM EST (optimal for business audience)
- **Engagement:** Respond to all comments within 4 hours

### Twitter/X Threads
- **Length:** 5-10 tweets per thread
- **Structure:** Hook → Value → Examples → CTA
- **Media:** Images, screenshots, brief videos
- **Timing:** 12:00 PM EST (midday engagement)
- **Hashtags:** 2-3 max, platform-appropriate
- **Engagement:** Like replies, respond to questions, retweet valuable additions

### Blog Articles
- **Length:** 1,500-2,500 words
- **Structure:** Problem → Solution → How-to → Examples → Next steps
- **SEO:** Target keywords, meta descriptions, internal linking
- **Media:** 3-5 images/screenshots, optional video
- **Promotion:** Social, email, community, potential syndication

### Email Content
- **Format:** HTML with plain text fallback
- **Length:** 300-500 words (short and scannable)
- **CTAs:** 1 primary, 1-2 secondary max
- **Personalization:** Name, company (if known), usage data
- **Timing:** 6:00 PM EST (evening reading)
- **Testing:** A/B test subject lines, CTAs, content length

### Community Content
- **Platforms:** Slack, Discord, Forum (depending on setup)
- **Format:** Conversational, engaging, collaborative
- **Frequency:** 2-3 posts per day minimum
- **Moderation:** Active but light-touch
- **Encouragement:** User-generated content, discussions, questions
- **Recognition:** Regular shoutouts, featured content, rewards

## Performance Metrics & Optimization

### Content Performance Metrics
| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| LinkedIn Engagement Rate | 3%+ | (Likes+Comments+Shares)/Impressions |
| Twitter Engagement Rate | 1.5%+ | (Likes+Retweets+Replies)/Impressions |
| Blog Read Time | 3+ minutes | Analytics avg time on page |
| Email Open Rate | 40%+ | Unique opens/Sent |
| Email Click Rate | 15%+ | Unique clicks/Sent |
| Content Conversions | 5%+ | Signups from content |
| Community Activity | 20%+ daily | Active members/Total members |

### Optimization Approach
1. **Daily Review:** What content performed best/worst?
2. **A/B Testing:** Subject lines, CTAs, content formats
3. **Audience Analysis:** Who's engaging vs who's not?
4. **Timing Optimization:** Best times for each platform
5. **Format Testing:** Video vs image vs text vs carousel
6. **Message Refinement:** Which messaging resonates most?

### Success Indicators
- **Early:** Engagement metrics meeting/exceeding targets
- **Mid-week:** Conversion metrics tracking to goal
- **End of week:** User retention and activity strong
- **Long-term:** Content creating sustainable growth

## Risk Mitigation

### Content Risks
- **Risk:** Content fatigue (too much, too similar)
- **Mitigation:** Vary formats, themes, timing; monitor engagement drop-off
- **Risk:** Inconsistent quality across channels
- **Mitigation:** Content calendar, approval workflow, quality checklist
- **Risk:** Negative feedback amplification
- **Mitigation:** Response protocols, transparency, constructive engagement

### Resource Risks
- **Risk:** Team capacity constraints
- **Mitigation:** Prioritize high-impact content, template creation, batch work
- **Risk:** Tool limitations/outages
- **Mitigation:** Backup tools, manual processes, rapid switching capability
- **Risk:** Content creation bottlenecks
- **Mitigation:** Pre-create content bank, reuse/repurpose content, user-generated content

### Performance Risks
- **Risk:** Low initial engagement
- **Mitigation:** Paid promotion for key content, influencer outreach, community seeding
- **Risk:** Poor conversion from content
- **Mitigation:** Clear CTAs, conversion tracking, iterative improvement
- **Risk:** Negative sentiment trends
- **Mitigation:** Rapid response, transparency, product improvements
