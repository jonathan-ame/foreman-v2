## Risk Mitigation

### High Risks (Monitor Closely)
1. **Case study delay** - CTO draft not ready today
   - **Mitigation:** CMO create minimal version as backup
   - **Trigger:** No draft by 12:00 PM EST

2. **Technical issues during launch** - Site performance problems
   - **Mitigation:** CTO stress testing, performance monitoring
   - **Trigger:** Load testing results

3. **Low initial traction** - Launch announcements not gaining traction
   - **Mitigation:** Paid amplification ready, influencer outreach
   - **Trigger:** Engagement <50% of target after 4 hours

### Medium Risks (Watch List)
4. **Team coordination gaps** - Miscommunication or role confusion
   - **Mitigation:** Clear documentation, daily standups, escalation paths
   - **Trigger:** Missed deadlines or unclear ownership

5. **Monitoring tool failures** - Alerts not working
   - **Mitigation:** Manual backup checks, multiple tool redundancy
   - **Trigger:** Missed mentions or delayed alerts

6. **Negative sentiment spike** - Criticism going viral
   - **Mitigation:** Response protocols, rapid escalation, transparent communication
   - **Trigger:** Negative sentiment >30% or viral negative post

### Low Risks (Monitor)
7. **Content scheduling issues** - Posts not publishing on time
   - **Mitigation:** Manual posting backup, scheduling tool redundancy
   - **Trigger:** Missed post schedule

8. **Email delivery problems** - Emails not reaching inboxes
   - **Mitigation:** Email platform testing, spam score monitoring
   - **Trigger:** Open rate <20% or bounce rate >5%
