# Foreman v2 - Financial Model Assumptions

## Overview
This document outlines the key assumptions for Foreman's financial projections. **CEO input required** for strategic direction, revenue targets, and funding needs.

## Revenue Model

### **Pricing Structure**
**Tier 1: Founder**
- Price: $49/month
- Target ACV: $588/year
- Included: 1 agent, basic workflows, 1k task/month
- Target Segment: Solopreneurs testing AI

**Tier 2: Growth**
- Price: $199/month  
- Target ACV: $2,388/year
- Included: 3 agents, advanced coordination, 5k tasks/month
- Target Segment: Small businesses scaling operations

**Tier 3: Scale**
- Price: $499/month
- Target ACV: $5,988/year
- Included: 10 agents, enterprise features, 20k tasks/month
- Target Segment: Established businesses with multiple needs

**Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) Target:** $200/month ($2,400/year)

### **Customer Mix Assumptions**
**Year 1 Target:**
- Founder Tier: 40% of customers (400 customers)
- Growth Tier: 40% of customers (400 customers)  
- Scale Tier: 20% of customers (200 customers)

**Resulting ARPU Calculation:**
```
(400 × $49) + (400 × $199) + (200 × $499) ÷ 1,000 = $199.60/month
```

### **Growth Trajectory**
**Monthly Customer Acquisition Targets:**
- **Month 1-3:** 25 new customers/month (ramp-up)
- **Month 4-6:** 75 new customers/month (growth)  
- **Month 7-9:** 150 new customers/month (scaling)
- **Month 10-12:** 250 new customers/month (optimized)

**Churn Rate Assumptions:**
- **Monthly Churn:** 5% (industry standard for SMB SaaS)
- **Annual Retention:** 54% (needs improvement target)
- **Expansion Revenue:** 20% of customers upgrade annually

## Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### **Key Risks:**
1. **Customer Acquisition Cost Higher Than Expected**
   - Mitigation: Focus on organic/content channels first
   - Buffer: Assume 20% higher CAC in worst case

2. **Churn Higher Than Expected**
   - Mitigation: Focus on customer success from day one
   - Buffer: Plan for 8% monthly churn vs. 5% target

3. **Revenue Per User Lower Than Expected**
   - Mitigation: Focus on Growth/Scale tiers from launch
   - Buffer: Assume $150 ARPU vs. $200 target
