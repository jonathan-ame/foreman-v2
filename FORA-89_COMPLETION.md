## ⚠️ Risks & Mitigations

**Monitoring Gaps Risk:** Missing important mentions on unmonitored platforms  
**Mitigation:** Broad keyword monitoring, manual daily checks, community alerts

**Tool Failure Risk:** Monitoring tool outage during critical period  
**Mitigation:** Redundant tools (free + paid), manual backup checks

**Alert Fatigue Risk:** Too many alerts causing important ones to be missed  
**Mitigation:** Tiered alert system, clear escalation rules, regular tuning

**Response Overload Risk:** Too many mentions to handle effectively  
**Mitigation:** Template responses, prioritization rules, team coverage schedule

**Sentiment Misreading Risk:** AI sentiment analysis incorrectly categorizing mentions  
**Mitigation:** Human review of key mentions, regular calibration, context-aware analysis
