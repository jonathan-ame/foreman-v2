## 🆘 **TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK GUIDE**

### **Issue: LinkedIn posts not getting engagement**
**Action:** 
1. Increase comment engagement (respond to all)
2. Share in relevant LinkedIn groups (if appropriate)
3. Ask team to engage with posts
4. Consider small promotion boost

### **Issue: Email open rate low**
**Action:**
1. Check subject line optimization
2. Verify sender reputation
3. Test different send times
4. Segment audience if possible

**Action:**
1. Check all UTM parameters
2. Verify landing pages load
3. Test on multiple devices
4. Contact CTO if persistent issues

**Action:**

2. Address concerns transparently
3. Escalate serious issues to CEO
4. Document feedback for product improvements
