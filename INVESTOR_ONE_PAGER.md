# Foreman v2 - Investor One-Pager

## Company Overview

**Foreman v2** is the AI agent orchestration platform built specifically for solopreneurs and small businesses (1-10 employees). We enable SMBs to deploy and manage AI agents without technical expertise, providing "your AI team member, not another SaaS tool."

**Headline:** No-code AI agents for small businesses that need to scale smart

**Tagline:** Scale your business with AI, not headcount

## The Problem

Small businesses face a critical gap in AI adoption:
- **75%** of SMBs want to use AI but **90%** lack technical resources
- Hiring technical staff costs **$60k+/year** with management overhead
- DIY AI development requires **months of work** + ongoing maintenance
- Current AI tools are either **too technical** (LangChain) or **too limited** (single-purpose tools)

**Result:** 25M+ US SMBs are stuck between expensive hiring and inaccessible technology.

## Our Solution

Foreman v2 provides no-code AI agent orchestration with managed operations:

**Core Value Proposition:**
1. **No-Code Setup:** Deploy AI agents in 15 minutes, no coding required
2. **Managed Operations:** We handle AI infrastructure, monitoring, and optimization
3. **Growth-Aligned Pricing:** Scales from $49/month to $499/month as businesses grow

**Key Differentiators:**
- **Built for SMBs:** Simple interface, business-focused agents, clear ROI
- **Enterprise Foundation:** Built on Paperclip (orchestration) + OpenClaw (runtime)
- **Coordinated Workflows:** Multiple agents working together, not isolated tools
