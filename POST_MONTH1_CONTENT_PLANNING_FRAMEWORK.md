# Post-Month 1 Content Planning Framework (June 2026+)

**Purpose:** Flexible content strategy framework for Months 2+ based on Month 1 launch results and learnings.

**Approach:** Data-driven planning with multiple scenario paths based on Month 1 performance.

**Timing:** Develop detailed Month 2 calendar after analyzing Week 4 (May 26-31) results.

---

## 🎯 **CONTENT STRATEGY OBJECTIVES (MONTHS 2+)**

### **Primary Goals:**
1. **Amplify Success:** Double down on high-performing Month 1 content themes
2. **Deepen Engagement:** Move from awareness to consideration/decision
3. **Build Community:** Foster user conversations and peer learning
4. **Showcase Results:** Highlight customer success stories and ROI
5. **Expand Use Cases:** Demonstrate broader application of AI agents

### **Success Metrics for Month 2:**
- **Engagement Growth:** 20%+ increase vs. Month 1 averages
- **Conversion Lift:** Higher waitlist-to-trial conversion rate
- **Content Amplification:** 30%+ of content generated from community/users
- **Brand Building:** Increased organic mentions and referrals

---

## 📊 **SCENARIO-BASED PLANNING**

### **Scenario A: Strong Traction (Exceeds Month 1 Targets)**
**Assumption:** Month 1 waitlist > 500, engagement > targets, positive sentiment

**Month 2 Focus:** **ACCELERATE & SCALE**
- **Content Ratio:** 40% success stories, 30% advanced use cases, 20% community, 10% product
- **Key Initiatives:**
  1. **Customer spotlight series:** Weekly featured user/case study
  2. **Advanced workflows:** Deep dives on complex agent coordination
  3. **Integration showcases:** Partner tool integrations
  4. **Community challenges:** User-generated content campaigns
  5. **Expert interviews:** AI/automation thought leaders

### **Scenario B: Moderate Traction (Meets Month 1 Targets)**
**Assumption:** Month 1 waitlist 200-500, engagement meets targets, neutral/positive sentiment

**Month 2 Focus:** **OPTIMIZE & DEEPEN**
- **Content Ratio:** 30% use cases, 30% education, 25% product, 15% community
- **Key Initiatives:**
  1. **Use case expansion:** Broaden application examples
  2. **Education series:** "AI Agents 101" for different ICP segments
  3. **Product refinement:** Address Month 1 feedback in content
  4. **Community building:** Foster early adopter discussions
  5. **Competitive differentiation:** Clear "vs. alternatives" content

### **Scenario C: Weak Traction (Below Month 1 Targets)**
**Assumption:** Month 1 waitlist < 200, engagement below targets, mixed sentiment

**Month 2 Focus:** **PIVOT & LEARN**
- **Content Ratio:** 40% problem-solving, 30% market education, 20% feedback, 10% product
- **Key Initiatives:**
  1. **Problem-focused content:** Address perceived barriers/objections
  2. **Market education:** Broader "why AI agents" narrative
  3. **Feedback incorporation:** Transparent "we're listening" updates
  4. **Simplified messaging:** Refine value proposition clarity
  5. **Channel testing:** Experiment with new content formats/channels

## 📈 **PERFORMANCE TRACKING & ADAPTATION**

### **Weekly Review Points:**
1. **Content Performance:** Top/bottom 3 posts analysis
2. **Audience Growth:** Follower trends, new segments
3. **Engagement Quality:** Comment depth, conversation threads
4. **Conversion Impact:** Traffic → waitlist correlation
5. **Sentiment Analysis:** Positive/negative theme identification

### **Adaptation Triggers:**
- **Positive trigger (accelerate):** Content achieving 150%+ of target engagement
- **Warning trigger (optimize):** Content at 70-100% of target engagement  
- **Pivot trigger (change):** Content below 70% of target engagement for 2+ weeks

### **A/B Testing Queue:**
1. **Headline variants:** Benefit-focused vs. problem-focused
2. **CTA variations:** Trial-focused vs. learning-focused
3. **Content length:** Short/quick reads vs. detailed deep dives
4. **Visual style:** Data-heavy vs. simple/clean
5. **Posting time:** Morning vs. afternoon vs. evening

### **Planning Cycle (Bi-weekly):**

- **Week 2, Tuesday-Wednesday:** Create/approve content

- **CMO:** Strategy, approval, performance analysis

- **Monthly theme:** Overarching focus area

- **Flexibility buffer:** 20% of content slots reserved for real-time opportunities
