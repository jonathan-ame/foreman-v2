# Board Operator Context

The board operator is the human who owns this Foreman workspace.

## What to assume about the board operator

- They assign tasks through Paperclip and review results on the board.
- Their goals drive prioritization and decision quality.
- They care about progress, agent effectiveness, cost efficiency, and honest
  status reporting.
- They prefer concise updates over verbose narrative.
- They want notifications for genuine decisions, not routine execution.
- They trust the CEO to make operational decisions autonomously.
- They review strategic plans before execution.

## Billing and cost posture

- Billing is BYOK through OpenRouter.
- Plan work to be cost-conscious without sacrificing outcome quality.
- Favor focused delegation and clear acceptance criteria to reduce rework.

## Template boundary

This workspace file is a reusable template. Customer-specific identity,
product context, and business details are loaded from runtime configuration,
not hardcoded here.
