# Launch Performance Metrics Framework

**Purpose**: Comprehensive measurement framework for May launch performance across engagement, conversion, and business impact.

**Scope**: 
- **Week 1 (Launch)** May 5-11: Awareness & acquisition
- **Weeks 2-4 (Post-launch)** May 12-31: Engagement & nurturing
- **Month 1+ (Impact)** June+: Business outcomes

**Measurement Frequency**:
- **Daily**: Engagement metrics
- **Weekly**: Performance summaries
- **Monthly**: Business impact analysis

**Data Sources**:
- LinkedIn Analytics
- Twitter Analytics
- Email platform metrics
- Google Analytics (website)
- Waitlist/CRM data
- Customer feedback

## 🎯 **SUCCESS THRESHOLDS & ADJUSTMENT CRITERIA**

### **Green Zone (Exceeding Targets)**
- **Action**: Accelerate successful tactics
- **Criteria**: 110%+ of target metrics
- **Examples**: Double down on top-performing content, expand budget for working channels

### **Yellow Zone (Meeting Targets)**
- **Action**: Optimize and test variations
- **Criteria**: 90-110% of target metrics
- **Examples**: A/B test headlines, refine targeting, improve CTAs

### **Red Zone (Below Targets)**
- **Action**: Pivot strategy
- **Criteria**: <90% of target metrics
- **Examples**: Test new content formats, adjust posting times, revisit audience targeting

### **Emergency Triggers**
| Trigger | Action | Owner |
|---------|--------|-------|
| **Engagement <50% target** | Content format test | CMO |
| **Click-through <1%** | CTA/landing page review | CMO/UXDesigner |
| **Negative sentiment spike** | Crisis response protocol | CMO/CEO |
| **Technical issues** | Immediate technical support | CTO |

## 🔍 **KEY PERFORMANCE INDICATORS (KPIs) DASHBOARD**

1. **Engagement Rate**: (% of impressions engaging)
2. **Click-Through Rate**: (% clicking links)
3. **Waitlist Conversion Rate**: (% of visitors signing up)

1. **Audience Growth Rate**: (% follower increase)
2. **Lead Velocity**: (New leads per week)
3. **Content Amplification**: (Shares per post)
4. **Conversation Quality**: (Depth of interactions)

### **Impact KPIs (Monthly Review)**
1. **Customer Acquisition Cost**: (Total spend / customers)
2. **Marketing-Sourced Revenue**: (Revenue from launch efforts)
3. **Brand Health Score**: (Composite of sentiment, awareness, consideration)
4. **Market Share Signals**: (Vs. competitive mentions)
